Reputation by Taylor Swift

June 4, 2018

This album took a while to grow on me, but I think that I can safely say that it is now my favorite Taylor Swift album. That's perhaps less impressive than it might seem if you consider the fact that I haven't been an active fan of hers since the Fearless era. However, this album accomplished the difficult task of making me an avid T. Swift fan once again, just like back in the golden days of 2009.
